Martin Luther King Jr

Martin Luther King Jr's Biggest Moments:

  • Born on January 15, 1929 in Atlanta Georgia.
  • Graduates from Morehouse College with a bachelor's degree in sociology on June 8,1948.
  • In January of 1952, King meets Corretta Scott, who he will soon marry.
  • King and Scott get married on June 18,1953.
  • September 20, 1958, King is stabbed with a seven-inch letter opener by a mentally ill woman.
  • On February 1, 1960, Martin Luther King moved from Montgomery to Atlanta to focus on the civil rights struggle.
  • On October 19, 1960, King is arrested for violating a suspended sentence in a 1956 traffic violation. He would later get arrested for other reasons, one of them being a desegregation camp.
  • August 28, 1963: Martin Luther King Jr says his famous "I Have Dream" speech in front of thousands of people at the Lincoln Memorial.
  • Then, on December 10, 1964 King wins a Nobel Peace Prize.
  • April 4 1968: King is assassinated on the balcony of a motel.
